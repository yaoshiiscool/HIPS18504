Riepel · 1755

Monte · Ponte · Fonte

A fixed opening phrase (bars 1–2) closes on the dominant. Choose one of Riepel's three continuation types for bars 3–4.

Monte

The phrase rises — a sequential progression moving upward by step.

Ponte

A bridge on the dominant — the melody holds around G.

Fonte

The phrase falls — a sequential progression moving downward by step.
