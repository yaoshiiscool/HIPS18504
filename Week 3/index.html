Scenarios

Learn how the bureau system works through real challenges inspired by de Prony's actual labor division.

📖 Scenario 1: Perfect Conditions ▼

Setup: Compute 10 sine values with 2 expert workers, crystal-clear instructions, and strict checking.

Goal: Achieve 100% accuracy and understand the ideal workflow.

🔥 Scenario 2: The Coordination Challenge ▼

Setup: Compute 20 sine values with 5 workers, unclear instructions, light checking, and time pressure.

Goal: Observe how adding workers without coordination support increases overhead and errors.

⚡ Scenario 3: Speed Trap ▼

Setup: Compute 25 sine values with 8 workers, moderate clarity, medium checking, and heavy pressure.

Goal: Test whether large teams can overcome the rush without collapsing into errors.

🧠 Scenario 4: Minimal Bureau ▼

Setup: Compute 15 sine values with 1 worker, clear instructions, strict checking, and mild pressure.

Goal: Compare a careful small bureau to a fast but error-prone team.
